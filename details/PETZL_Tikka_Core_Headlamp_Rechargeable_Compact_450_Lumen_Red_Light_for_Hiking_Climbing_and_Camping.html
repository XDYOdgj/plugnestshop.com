PETZL Tikka Core Headlamp: Trusted Lighting for Every Adventure

Whether you're hiking through dense forests, scaling steep mountain trails, or camping under the stars, the PETZL Tikka Core Headlamp delivers the performance you need to stay safe and confident in the dark.

This headlamp is designed with a 50:50 brightness-to-burn-time ratio, ensuring that you maintain 50% brightness right up to the halfway point of its battery life. That means more time in the wilderness without worrying about sudden power loss.

With a rechargeable system and the flexibility to use either the CORE battery or standard AAA batteries, the headlamp is both eco-friendly and convenient. Its compact, lightweight build ensures it fits effortlessly into your backpack or jacket, making it perfect for outdoor enthusiasts always on the move.

The full-tilt design lets you adjust the light to your exact needs, whether you're wearing it around your neck or using it hands-free. Plus, the integrated micro USB port allows you to charge the CORE battery while it's still in use, saving you time and hassle.

Equipped with a red light mode for low-light visibility, this headlamp is also ideal for night hiking and emergency situations. The intuitive features, like the battery indicator and glow-in-the-dark reflector, ensure you're always aware of your remaining power and stay visible in the dark.

From backyard adventures to long-distance treks, the PETZL Tikka Core Headlamp is built to last. Its sleek, ergonomic design and reliable performance make it a must-have for your outdoor gear collection.
